The Problem

AI tools are advancing exponentially. The creative explosion is real. But something unexpected is happening: when everyone uses the same tools, output collapses toward the mean.

Your imperfections are not weaknesses. They are your Creative Fingerprint: your perspective on what needs to exist, based on your entire lived experience. No one can copy it. This work is about capturing, transmitting, and amplifying that fingerprint through AI, not despite AI.

Everything here comes from working alongside people. Designers, creators, teams. Not theory. Practice and application.

30,000+ experiments in co-cognition proved that human nuance can be captured, transmitted, and amplified through frontier AI rather than averaged out. The work operates at three scales: individual creators preserving their fingerprints, teams maintaining collective originality, and organizations building competitive advantage through authentic human-AI collaboration.

Exploring four shifts

Humans How we evolve when AI becomes our creative partner. The challenge is not learning the tool. It is training our minds to think alongside it.

Frameworks & Workshops

Systematic approaches for preserving originality in human-AI work

Collaborations and workshops mapping how teams and organizations can transmit their creative DNA through AI systems while maintaining strategic differentiation. The approach is experimental. Not theorizing but building. Not predicting but discovering what actually happens when two intelligences meet.

Vibe Designing

Maps how creative intent travels through AI systems. A framework for translating the prelinguistic signals, the gut feelings and aesthetic instincts, into structured guidance that AI can actually work with. Developed through practice with design teams navigating AI-augmented workflows.

Copyself Protocol

A systematic approach for capturing and transmitting your creative identity through any AI system. Not your style. Your cognitive signature: the way you see, decide, and make. Built from 30,000+ experiments in co-cognition.

Creative Fingerprint Transmission

Preserves what makes your work yours at scale. How teams and organizations encode their collective creative DNA so it survives and strengthens through AI collaboration rather than averaging out.

Bio

My field of work is cognitive architecture: the inner workings of the human mind and the underlying cognitive mechanisms behind how humans interact with AI systems.

Everything I know comes from working with people. Not theory. Practice.

From working with 250 designers on AI transformation at Logitech, to designing the supercomputers at Cerebras that train frontier models, to figuring out what AI-native interfaces need to become. One continuous thread: the cognitive layer between people and intelligent systems.

How do we capture, transmit, and amplify human creativity through AI systems so that people become more authentic, more original, not less? That is the work.
